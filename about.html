Who We Are

Wecott is a modern platform where you can create your own sub-websites for events, protests, or any initiative. We are dedicated to providing the tools you need to get your message out there.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of organizing. Whether you want to create a movement, hold a community event, or organize a protest, Wecott gives you the platform to host your own sub-site, manage your participants, and communicate your message effectively.
